EVIDENCE MODE — PRIMARY-SOURCE-HEAVY

Prioritize:
- official filings
- transcripts
- speeches
- budgets
- official statistics
- product documentation
- court records
- primary disclosures

Strengths:
- defensible factual base

Weaknesses:
- may lag reality
- may omit hidden behavior

## Stage Relevance

Routing — Primary
- Selects a primary-source-heavy proof style.

Boundary — Light
- Can slightly narrow scope toward what can be evidenced directly.

Structure — Modulating
- Keeps mechanism claims close to observable and documented evidence.

Scenarios — Modulating
- Prefers branches that can be justified by direct disclosures and formal behavior.

Question Generation — Primary
- Favors questions answerable through filings, transcripts, official statistics, or direct disclosures.

Evidence Planning — Primary
- Sets the hierarchy toward direct documents and revealed formal behavior.

Decision Logic — Modulating
- Raises the action threshold when primary evidence is expected before committing.

Signal Translation — Primary
- Favors signals from guidance changes, filings, official language, and formal disclosures.

Monitoring — Modulating
- Monitoring should emphasize official updates and disclosed behavior, while noting lag risk.

Render — Light
- Usually warrants explicit source hierarchy and source-quality rationale.
