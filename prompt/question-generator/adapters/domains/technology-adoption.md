## Stage Relevance

Routing — Primary
- Classifies the problem as a technology diffusion, substitution, or adoption system.

Boundary — Primary
- Pulls in complements, infrastructure, standards, switching costs, and diffusion channels.

Structure — Primary
- Makes cost curves, reliability, interoperability, trust, and ecosystem readiness central structural elements.

Scenarios — Primary
- Favors diffusion, stall, substitution, standards split, and complement bottleneck branches.

Question Generation — Primary
- Favors bottleneck, complement, threshold, cost/performance, and user-behavior questions.

Evidence Planning — Primary
- Prefers benchmark, deployment, developer, pilot, and retention evidence.

Decision Logic — Modulating
- Shapes what action means: adoption timing, platform bet, ecosystem sequencing, and staged commitment.

Signal Translation — Primary
- Signals should emphasize deployment rates, benchmark improvement, developer activity, integrations, and retention after trial.

Monitoring — Primary
- Monitoring should track diffusion speed, standards adoption, complement readiness, and usage persistence.

Render — Light
- Mainly affects domain vocabulary and examples.
