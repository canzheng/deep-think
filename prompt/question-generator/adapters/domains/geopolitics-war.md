## Stage Relevance

Routing — Primary
- Classifies the problem as a geopolitical or conflict system with state, coalition, and escalation logic.

Boundary — Primary
- Pulls in adjacent systems like alliances, logistics, energy, finance, and domestic politics.

Structure — Primary
- Makes states, leaders, militaries, factions, and coalitions the natural actors and bottlenecks.

Scenarios — Primary
- Favors escalation, de-escalation, frozen conflict, coalition fracture, and endurance branches.

Question Generation — Primary
- Favors questions about endurance, coercion, coalition cohesion, logistics, and costly signals.

Evidence Planning — Primary
- Prefers official behavior, primary statements, field indicators, and logistics evidence over pure narrative.

Decision Logic — Modulating
- Shapes what intervention, leverage, deterrence, or exposure management means in this domain.

Signal Translation — Primary
- Signals should emphasize mobilization, strike patterns, diplomacy, sanctions, and elite behavior.

Monitoring — Primary
- Monitoring should track escalation ladders, logistics endurance, and coalition changes.

Render — Light
- Mainly affects vocabulary and domain examples rather than structure by itself.
