TASK ADAPTER — DESIGN / INFLUENCE

Prioritize:
- levers
- intervention points
- second-order effects
- implementation constraints
- stakeholder reactions

## Stage Relevance

Routing — Primary
- Sets the overall job to shaping outcomes rather than merely describing them.

Boundary — Primary
- Pulls scope toward levers, intervention points, and implementation constraints.

Structure — Primary
- Prioritizes leverage structure, response dynamics, and second-order effects.

Scenarios — Primary
- Requires scenarios that show how interventions change path outcomes and stakeholder reactions.

Question Generation — Primary
- Favors action-shaping, intervention-design, and reaction-risk questions.

Evidence Planning — Modulating
- Prefers evidence on leverage, feasibility, response, and implementation friction.

Decision Logic — Modulating
- Matters whenever influence requires sequencing, staging, or reversible interventions.

Signal Translation — Modulating
- Signals should indicate whether interventions are working or backfiring.

Monitoring — Modulating
- Monitoring should watch implementation milestones, stakeholder reactions, and second-order effects.

Render — Primary
- Pushes the final artifact toward strategy, levers, and intervention logic.
