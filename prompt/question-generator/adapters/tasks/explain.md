TASK ADAPTER — EXPLAIN

Prioritize:
- sequence reconstruction
- trigger vs cause
- dominant mechanism
- alternative explanations
- what mattered most

## Stage Relevance

Routing — Primary
- Sets the overall job to explanation and makes causal understanding the success criterion.

Boundary — Primary
- Pulls scope toward causes of what already happened and the time frame needed to explain them.

Structure — Primary
- Prioritizes causal structure, mechanism, trigger, and alternative explanation analysis.

Scenarios — Modulating
- Uses scenarios mainly as rival explanations or counterfactual paths, not as forward forecasts.

Question Generation — Primary
- Favors causal attribution questions and questions that distinguish trigger from deep cause.

Evidence Planning — Modulating
- Prefers chronology, sequence, and mechanism-validating evidence.

Decision Logic — Light
- Only matters if explanation is meant to inform a later action.

Signal Translation — Light
- Signals mainly indicate whether the explanation is supported or falsified.

Monitoring — Light
- Monitoring is limited to what would confirm that the explanation remains valid.

Render — Primary
- Pushes the final artifact toward explanatory clarity rather than action or prediction.
